Request account & data deletion

The fastest way to delete your account is in-app, under Settings → Account → Delete Account. If you can't access the app, you can request deletion here:

Email support@tecknack.co.uk from the address linked to your account, with the subject line "Delete my account," and tell us which app you used and your in-app display name. We'll confirm receipt and complete deletion of your account and associated personal data within 5 working days, except where we're required to retain certain records — see our Privacy Policy for details.

Frequently asked questions

How do I cancel a subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID (Settings → your name → Subscriptions) or your Google Play account (Play Store → Subscriptions), not through us directly. Cancelling there stops future renewals.

I think I was charged twice — what do I do?

Duplicate or unexpected charges are resolved through Apple or Google's own purchase history and refund tools, since they handle billing. If you're not sure where to start, email support@tecknack.co.uk with the app name and approximate date, and we'll help you find the right report.

How do I delete my account and data?

Use the in-app "Delete Account" option under Settings, or email privacy@tecknack.co.uk as described above. See the deletion section on this page for details.

Is this app safe for kids?

Some of our apps and games are designed for a general audience, and some are built specifically with younger players in mind — each app's store listing says which it is. Apps directed at children follow the additional protections described in Section 7 of our Privacy Policy, including no behaviorally-targeted advertising.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Email support@tecknack.co.uk with the app name, your device and OS version, and what you expected to happen versus what actually happened — screenshots help a lot.
